Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how Bambinoo (“we”, “our”, “the system”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your data when you use our Child Health & Growth Monitoring System powered by AI.

1. Information We Collect

  • Child Information: name, date of birth, height, weight, BMI, Z-scores, vaccination history, clinical notes.
  • Parent/Guardian Information: name, contact number, email, and relationship to the child.
  • Healthcare Staff Information: full name, role, professional ID, login records.
  • System Usage Data: IP address, device type, browser, cookies, activity logs.
  • AI Interaction Data: chat history, query inputs, recommended outputs for auditing and improvements.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Generate accurate health analytics, including BMI and WHO growth Z-scores.
  • Provide AI-powered recommendations for food, nutrition, and exercise.
  • Enable parents to communicate with doctors and nurses.
  • Send vaccination reminders, alerts, and growth updates.
  • Improve system performance, accuracy, and security.

3. Data Storage & Security

All personal information is securely stored using industry-standard encryption. Only authorized medical staff and system administrators have access based on role permission levels.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share data only with:

  • Authorized doctors, nurses, and midwives.
  • Government health authorities (only if legally required).
  • Secure third-party service providers such as hosting or analytics partners.

We never sell personal data.

5. Children’s Data Protection

We give the highest priority to children’s privacy. Parents or legal guardians control and manage their child's information and can request updates or deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

  • Request correction of your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your account or child profile.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (where applicable).
  • Access the data we store about you.
