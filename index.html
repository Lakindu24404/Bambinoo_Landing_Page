Our Aim & Mission

Our Aim

Create a digitalised child health monitoring record system that helps parents monitor the growth and development of their children while making the lives of doctors and nurses/midwives easier by allowing them to analyse and record child data more efficiently.

Our Mission

Ensure proactive child health care with AI-driven monitoring, nutrition and exercise guidance, vaccination alerts, and digital medical records. We empower families and healthcare professionals with intelligent tools for optimal child development.

Q&A — Common Questions

Everything you need to know about Bambinoo

How does Bambinoo track child growth?

Bambinoo uses AI algorithms to analyze weight, height, and developmental milestones entered by parents or healthcare providers. The system compares data against WHO growth standards and provides percentile rankings and predictive insights.

Is AI nutrition advice medically verified?

Yes, all nutrition recommendations are developed by certified pediatric nutritionists and reviewed by medical professionals. The AI provides suggestions based on established dietary guidelines for children.

Can I chat with doctors through the platform?

The AI chatbot provides immediate answers to common questions. For specific medical concerns, you can request consultations with healthcare providers through the platform's telemedicine feature (available in premium plans).

Are vaccination reminders automatic?

Yes, once you enter your child's birth date, Bambinoo automatically schedules vaccination reminders based on recommended immunization schedules. You'll receive notifications via SMS, email, and in-app alerts.

Can staff update CHDR records on mobile?

Absolutely! Healthcare providers can access and update child health records through our mobile app with proper authentication, making clinic visits more efficient and reducing paperwork.

How secure is the data?

We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) and comply with international healthcare data protection standards. All data is stored securely in the cloud with regular backups and strict access controls.

Does it work offline?

Basic features like viewing stored records and entering new data work offline. The information syncs automatically when you reconnect to the internet. AI features require an internet connection.

Who prepares educational content?

Our educational materials are created by pediatricians, child psychologists, and certified educators, then reviewed by our medical advisory board to ensure accuracy and relevance.

Is the chatbot available 24/7?

Yes! The AI chatbot is available around the clock to answer common parenting and health questions. For urgent medical issues, the system will recommend contacting emergency services or your healthcare provider.

Can I add multiple children to one account?

Yes, you can manage multiple children's profiles under a single parent account. Each child has their own separate health records, vaccination schedules, and growth tracking.
