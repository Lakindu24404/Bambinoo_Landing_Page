Our Aim & Mission

Our Aim

Create a digitalized child health monitoring record system that helps parents monitor the growth and development of their children while making the lives of doctors and nurses/midwives easier by allowing them to analyze and record child data more efficiently

Our Mission

Empower parents and health workers with a simple, secure digital child-health record that tracks growth, flags risks early, and turns real-time child data into faster, smarter care.

Q&A — Common Questions

Everything you need to know about Bambinoo

How does Bambinoo track child growth?

Bambinoo uses AI algorithms to analyze weight, height, and developmental milestones entered by parents or healthcare providers. The system compares data against WHO growth standards and provides percentile rankings and predictive insights.

Are AI-powered meal plans personalized?

Yes, meal suggestions are generated based on your child's age, weight, allergies, and progress. The AI analyzes CHDR data and adjusts recommendations, such as focusing on protein for underweight children, using WHO guidelines.

Are notifications and alerts automatic?

Yes, CHDR sends customizable push, email, or SMS reminders for vaccinations, check-ups, and events based on staff-set schedules. Mothers can adjust preferences, and staff can manage group alerts efficiently.

Does it work offline?

Basic features like viewing stored records and entering new data work offline. The information syncs automatically when you reconnect to the internet. AI features require an internet connection.

How does AI enhance analytics for doctors?

AI provides predictive growth charts forecasting metrics against WHO standards, recovery suggestions for deviations, and risk assessments scoring issues like malnutrition. It processes CHDR data optionally, delivering actionable insights to improve child health interventions.

What role does the admin panel play in system oversight?

The admin panel handles staff registration with role assignments, system configurations like AI thresholds, audit logs for actions, and full portal access for impersonation. It also manages integrations with national systems.

Who prepares educational content?

Our educational like videos, articles, and reports on milestones, and emergency care are curated from reliable sources such as the Family Health Bureau. AI personalizes recommendations based on your child's risk level.

Can I request reports through the platform?

Yes, mothers can submit requests for physical or digital reports. Track status in real-time, and staff generate them with charts for easy verification.

Can I add multiple children to one account?

Yes, you can manage multiple children's profiles under a single parent account. Each child has their own separate health records, vaccination schedules, and growth tracking.
